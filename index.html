how the bot works

The mayhem bot is funded by $MAYHEMFUN trading fees. When you enable mayhem mode on your coin, the bot starts buying. Here's exactly what it does:

01 — ENTRY
🤖
Bot buys in
The bot uses accumulated $MAYHEMFUN fees to market buy your coin. Real on-chain volume from the moment mayhem mode activates.
02 — LOCK
⏱️
2 minute strict lock
After a buy, the bot enforces a strict 2-minute lock — no selling, no reacting, just letting the position breathe and settle into the chart.
03 — FLOW
🌊
Rhythm-based execution
Once the lock expires, it doesn't dump. Sales go out in controlled slices — 10–25% at a time — adapting to what the chart is doing in real time.
04 — DISTRIBUTE
💸
Profits to holders
Profits are automatically distributed to $MAYHEMFUN holders. Hold the token, earn from every coin the bot trades.
adaptive sell behavior
volume is high, buys keep coming
→ leans in, distributes more into strength
momentum slows
→ holds back, sells lighter and slower
chart heats up
→ scales out more aggressively into liquidity
more buys → more volume
→ more opportunity to sell cleanly
it's not time-based dumping — it's rhythm-based execution
The bot reads the flow. Instead of killing charts, it feeds into them. More buys = more volume = more opportunity to exit cleanly. Mayhem mode is designed to add momentum, not end it.
